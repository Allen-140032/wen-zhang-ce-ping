> Paste your WeChat article, get a 6-dimension score with actionable improvement tips.

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

## What is this?

A pure frontend tool that analyzes WeChat public account articles across 6 dimensions:

- **Title Appeal** — Does it trigger curiosity?
- **Opening Hook** — Does the first paragraph grab attention?
- **Paragraph Rhythm** — Is the pacing right for mobile reading?
- **Emotional Curve** — Does the article have emotional ups and downs?
- **Readability** — Is it easy to read on phones?
- **Call to Action** — Does the ending encourage engagement?

Each dimension scored 1-10, total 60 points.
